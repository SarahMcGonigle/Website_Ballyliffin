The Rachtan Bar

A superb Bar Food Menu is available daily with a wide selection of the finest local fresh seafood, meats and seasonal produce, prepared to order and served by our friendly and efficient staff. The cosy traditional bar surroundings and comfortable lounges are the perfect backdrop in the evening time for lively entertainment and banter.


The Cruckaughrim Restaurant

For more formal dining and special celebrations, The Cruckaughrim Restaurant offers guests excellent service, wonderful food and great atmosphere.

Choose from sumptuous A la Carte and Table d’hôte menus carefully selected to bring you the very best of local fresh produce. Famous for Chargrilled Steaks and melt-in-the-mouth Baked Salmon, there’s something for everyone including a tempting selection of home-made desserts, liqueur coffees and house wines.
